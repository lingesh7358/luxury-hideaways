About Luxury Hideaways

Luxury Hideaways Travel & Tourism LLC is a boutique Destination Management Company (DMC) based in Dubai, specializing in curated luxury holiday packages. With a passion for crafting unforgettable journeys, we bring together India's most exclusive resorts, immersive experiences, and seamless services—designed especially for discerning travelers from the GCC region.

From the tranquil backwaters of Kerala to the royal palaces of Rajasthan, from Ayurvedic wellness retreats to gourmet culinary trails, each itinerary is handpicked to reflect the elegance, authenticity, and richness of India's heritage.

At Luxury Hideaways, we don't just offer travel—we curate timeless experiences with a signature touch of excellence. Whether you're planning a romantic escape, a bespoke family vacation, or a corporate incentive trip, our dedicated team ensures every detail is tailored to perfection.
